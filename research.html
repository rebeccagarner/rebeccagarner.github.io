Rebecca Garner

Research CV

1. Microbial eukaryotic diversity in Canadian lakes

Tracing the biogeography of microeukaryotes in the surface waters of lakes across Canada using molecular genetic techniques.

2. Clarifying the effects of eutrophication on microeukaryotic communities: Insights from the Experimental Lakes Area

Combining water column filtrate and sediment core time series.

3. Comparative paleogenetics of preindustrial and contemporary microeukaryotic assemblages from Canadian lake sediments

Assessing the impact of human activities on microeukaryotic communities after ~150 years of impact.

4. Revealing the undiscovered microbial diversity preserved in lake sediment DNA archives

With whole-metagenome shotgun sequencing.
