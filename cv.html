Research CV

Profile

I am a Ph.D. candidate under the mentorship of David Walsh at Concordia University and Irene Gregory-Eaves at McGill University. Driven by my fascination with microbial diversity, I am attempting to chart the preindustrial and contemporary diversity of microorganisms in lakes across Canada, with a special focus on microbial eukaryotes. Aside from microbial ecology, I am interested in molecular biology, computational biology, metagenomics, paleoecology, and reproducible science. Before beginning university, I volunteered for two and a half years of service in the Caracal Battalion of the Israel Defense Forces, which at the time was the only army combat unit in the world with equal gender integration.
