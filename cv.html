Profile

  • I am a Ph.D. candidate under the mentorship of David Walsh at Concordia University and Irene Gregory-Eaves at McGill University (links to Walsh and Gregory-Eaves labs). Driven by my fascination with microbial ecology and evolution, I am attempting to chart the contemporary and preindustrial diversity of microorganisms in lakes across Canada using culture-independent molecular and computational biology approaches and by developing paleogenomic techniques. Before beginning university, I volunteered for two and a half years of service in the Caracal Battalion of the Israel Defense Forces, which at the time was the only army combat unit in the world with equal gender integration.
